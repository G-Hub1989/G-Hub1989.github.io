Why 3D Printed Jewelry?

ED Designs was created out of an interest and passion to explore new ways of creating jewelry. The design process utilizes the capabilities of 3d printing technology, to quickly create several prototypes and get the design tweaked just right. The inspiration behind all of the designs stems from my architectural background, encompassing my interest in geometric patterns and its ability to create complexity and depth that can easily be turned into wearable art pieces. Each piece is designed to spice up any outfit, to make a unique and bold statement.
The parts are 3d printed using PLA filament, which is a fully biodegradable thermoplastic polymer consisting of renewable raw materials. The fine lines of the layers, each one less than a quarter of a millimeter thickness adds a dimensional quality to each piece. The result is a very lightweight piece of jewelry, perfect for everyday use.
